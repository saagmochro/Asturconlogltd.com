About Us

Founded in 2014, Astur Complex Construction & Logistic Supply Company is a privately owned enterprise headquartered in Kismayo, Jubbaland State of Somalia with an additional office in Nairobi.


We specialize in complex construction, roads, bridges, drainage systems, concrete works, irrigation canals, water works, and energy & solar plants.


Our expertise also spans heavy machinery supply and delivering projects that meet international standards while maintaining strong local collaboration .


With one of the largest pools of technical talent in the region, we pride ourselves on driving infrastructure development and community empowerment.


At Astur Conlog, we believe every successful project begins with purpose. We are not only building infrastructure but also rebuilding trust, transferring immerse skills, restoring dignity, and creating opportunities for future generations.


Our approach is rooted in empowering local talent, promoting innovation, and ensuring that every solution we deliver contributes to long-term community development.
